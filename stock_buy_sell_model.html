How this model works

This is a compact browser-based structural decision model, not financial advice. It is designed as a transparent scoring model you can customize. It first estimates intermediate structural pressures, then transforms the final scores into probabilities using a softmax function.

Momentum = scaled 6-month growth Risk pressure = scaled volatility Dollar pressure = dollar dependence × weekly dollar change Buy score = + momentum - risk pressure + dollar alignment Sell score = - momentum + risk pressure - dollar alignment Hold score = penalty for unclear signal
